About the Game

Close Call Extreme is a focused arcade racing game built around one simple idea: driving dangerously close isn't a mistake... It's how you survive.

Chain near misses, and chase higher scores in short, intense runs inspired by classic arcade racers.

Features

  • High-speed arcade racing
  • Close call thrill
  • Fast, replayable runs
  • Retro-inspired visuals and feel
  • Score chasing and leaderboard competition
